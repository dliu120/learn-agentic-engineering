RAG is a funnel, not a magic box: chunk and embed the corpus, pull semantic and keyword matches, fuse and rerank a small set, and only a handful of passages reach the model. Prove you can connect each stage to its failure mode and its fix.

<KeyInsightCallout>
The model can only reason over what retrieval handed it — recall sets the ceiling for the whole system, everything downstream is precision and ordering, and grounded, correct, and well-cited remain three separate questions.
</KeyInsightCallout>
